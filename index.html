Entertainment - Extreme

-

Central Park Boat House

After getting off at Songdo Central Park, walk toward the East Boat House. There, you will find the first seawater park in Korea. Various sports facilities are available, such as, canoes, kayaks, and electric boats. In the West Boat House, a water taxi is operated every 30 minutes that goes around the watercourse of the Central Park.

Monster VR

This is by far the biggest urban virtual reality theme park in Korea with 40 different VR content experiences. That's four times the average number of VR content in theme parks around the world. There is something for everyone. Visitors can jump through time and space, ride a rollercoaster, bungee-jump in a rainforest, or even fight zombies, become a ninja, or swim through the sea in Guam.

Entertainment - Energetic

-

Central Park Bike

Enjoy Central Park's scenery as you pedal along comfortably at your own pace with your Central Park bike rental. For inland activities, visitors can ride the couple bicycle or group bicycle in the Songdo Central Park.

-

Central Park: Love Island

n the Songdo Central Park is a boat sailing to the ‘Love Island’, located in the middle of the park. On the island is a place where a couple can stop and put a “love lock”. Also, the heart-shaped sculpture and benches make the atmosphere perfect for lovers to have special moments for just the two of them.

Entertainment - Relaxing

-

Mr. Healing

Mr. Healing bills itself as a relaxation cafe, a place where people can come in, order a drink and lie down a comfortable massage chair, where they can even take a short nap to the soothing sound of ambient or classical music, with pleasant and relaxing scents inundating their senses.

-

Megabox

Megabox is a chain of movie theatres in South Korea. Megabox Songdo has 8 screens with total 1,548 premium seats. There is a MX screen theater which will give you bigger impression. Megabox Songdo also offers "classic society" program which plays the most loved movies in 20th century.

Park - To Downtown

To Downtown

Have you ever thought that taking a taxi or a bus is just not worth the money to get to the place that you want to go to? Or perhaps it is your first week in Songdo and you are not quite sure how to use the buses or taxis to get to downtown? Well, there is some good news! Between IGC Global Campus and Downtown a.k.a. Dream City there are local parks scattered across the area. These parks make the 50 minute stroll to downtown a breeze.
From the IGC Campus to Downtown, the route above is worth a go around⤴

To Downtown

The main park that is a must see is Haedoji Park. This park is like a home for many residents of Songda as people at all times are running laps on the Parks track or reading books at the local library located with in the Park.

Park - Central Park

To Central Park

Central Park is the well known park of the Songdo Area. Although it is considered as local to many nearyby residents, Central Park's charms atracts visitors from all over Incheon. There are many nearby hotels around Central Park, so tourists from all over world as well are often seen at the park enjoying the aesthatic scenery that Central Park has to offer.
From the IGC Campus to Central Park, this route up here will bring you joy and cheer⤴

To Central Park

Central Park at night is an experience that is one to be cherished. Walking through the lonely shadows from the sparsed light poles or basking in the bright colors of lights from nearby buildings and bridges touches the heart in a way that somehow makes a stroll through Central Park a healing experience. Whether alone you care to explore and contemplate about Songdo's hidden mysteries or whether with others you wish to share a lovely scenery, Central Park will most definately not disapoint.
